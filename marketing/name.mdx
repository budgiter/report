## Meaning

Budgiter is a combination of the English word [budget](#budget) and the Latin word [iter](#iter)

### Budget

Dictionary definitions of the word budget

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="noun">
    an estimate of income and expenditure for a set period of time
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="verb">
    allow or provide a particular amount of money in a budget
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="wiki">
    a plan for a defined period, often one year or a month
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
